Who is Naveen Venkat?

Building the Future of Chip Design

I'm Naveen Venkat, Co-founder at ArchGen. We're building AI agents infrastructure to build the future of semiconductor chip design and accelerate the pipeline to build and ship these chips.

Before ArchGen, I was a Machine Learning Engineer at AB InBev (Budweiser), where I built agentic GenAI solutions, personalization engines, and optimization models across markets in South Africa and Latin America.

I've won the AB InBev Global Hackathon twice (2024 & 2025) and helped organize NITKonnect, connecting over 1,200 alumni and entrepreneurs from my alma mater.

Outside work, I co-host the SillyPoint Podcast where I rant about cricket and tech, and I'm always chasing my next running personal best.
